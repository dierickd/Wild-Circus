About Us

1955

Creation of Wild Circus by the Wildon family in Belgium. The family circus consisted of six people: Mrs. and Mr. Wildon, their two children and two coaches.

1962

First French tour, it was the real start in all of France, it was named "Le grand Wild" for the occasion.

1970

For their 15th anniversary, the Wild Circus is offering itself a big top with a capacity of 200 people. The company now has 30 members. With 12 performances per week and more than 30 issues, Wild Circus confirms its place in the field.

1990

The Wild Circus circus receives the Grand Prix National du cirque. Belgium will celebrate the event during a weekend of performance offered by the circus.
